Native American History:

    Origins:

    Native Americans lived in North America for over 15,000 years. They had about 562 tribes. Their control over the land would take a turn for the worse starting in 1492 when Christopher Columbus set foot on a Caribbean Island that he believed to be the East Indies. Once he landed, he took 6 Natives against their will and turned them into his servents. In 1513 Spaniards land in Florida and make contact with native americans and try to colonize them.
