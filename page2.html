African American History:

    Origins:

    African American's story began in Africa around 1526 when Europeans no longer wanted to use identured servants to work on the land in North America. They chose to steal African's from their land and force them into slave labor in North America. 20 Africans were shipped to Jamestown, Virginia and slavery quickly grew from there. You can read more about this by clicking this page. This page shows the whole timeline of African American history from 1526 to current.
