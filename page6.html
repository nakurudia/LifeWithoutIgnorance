Native Hawaiian or pacific Islander American History:

    Origins:

    Native Hawaiian or pacific Islander Americans migration to the United States happened between the 18th and 19th centuries. Fur traders were the first to migrate to the United States. They were only able to stay until their contracts were up and they had to be sent back to Hawaii. 1811 was when the firts Hawaii native was permanently.     • Pivotal Moments in Native Hawaiian or pacific Islander American History:

  • Started forming communities in California in 1830's
  • U.S. occupied Hawaii in 1896
  • U.S. occupied Guam in 1898
  • In 1959, Hawaii became a U.S. state
